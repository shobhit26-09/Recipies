Chocolate Mug Cake

Description

Chocolate Mug Cake is a quick and easy dessert made in the microwave. It’s soft, fluffy, and perfect for satisfying chocolate cravings in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Stir until smooth and well combined.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips, if using.
  5. Microwave on high for 1 minute to 1 minute 30 seconds.
  6. Let it cool for a minute before serving.
  7. Enjoy warm with ice cream or whipped cream.
