Garlic Butter Shrimp

Description

Garlic Butter Shrimp is a simple yet flavorful dish made with shrimp, garlic, butter, and a splash of lemon juice. It's quick to prepare and perfect for a delicious appetizer or a main dish served with pasta or rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat and melt the butter.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp and season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  4. Cook for 2–3 minutes on each side until shrimp turn pink.
  5. Stir in lemon juice and cook for another minute.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped parsley.
  7. Serve hot with pasta, rice, or toasted bread.
