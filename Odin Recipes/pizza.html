Margherita Pizza

Description

Margherita Pizza is a classic Italian pizza topped with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil. It has a simple yet rich flavor, making it one of the most loved pizzas worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 6-8 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Place mozzarella slices on top.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle salt, pepper, and oregano.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven and add fresh basil leaves.
  8. Slice and serve warm.
